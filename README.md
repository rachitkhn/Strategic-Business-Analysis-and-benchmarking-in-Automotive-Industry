---

## 📌 Project Overview

The automotive industry operates in a highly competitive, capital-intensive environment where strategic decisions must be grounded in rigorous financial data. This project delivers a comprehensive **Financial Overview Dashboard** covering 10 years of key performance metrics for a major automotive company, alongside competitive benchmarking against industry peers.

The analysis integrates market intelligence, financial modelling, and visualisation to surface actionable insights for executive leadership — covering pricing strategy, cost efficiency, technology investment, and competitive positioning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Build a dynamic, decade-long financial performance dashboard for executive reporting
- Conduct market and competitor analysis to contextualise company performance
- Benchmark key financial metrics against industry standards
- Deliver data-backed strategic recommendations for technology integration and cost optimisation
- Demonstrate proficiency in BI tools, statistical analysis, and business strategy

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The **Financial Overview Dashboard** consolidates 10 years of data across the following strategic dimensions:

### 💰 Financial Performance Metrics
- Revenue trends and year-on-year growth rates
- Profit margins (gross, operating, net) over the decade
- EBITDA analysis and cost structure breakdown
- Capital expenditure vs. R&D investment over time

### 📈 Market & Competitive Benchmarking
- Market share trends relative to key competitors
- Pricing strategy analysis across vehicle segments
- Sales volume performance by region and product line
- Competitor activity and product portfolio shifts

### ⚙️ Operational Efficiency
- Cost per unit production trends
- Supply chain efficiency indicators
- Manufacturing capacity utilisation rates
- Workforce productivity metrics

### 🔮 Strategic Insights
- Technology investment ROI analysis
- Identification of underperforming segments requiring strategic intervention
- Correlation between R&D spend and market share growth

---

## 🔬 Methodology

### 1. Data Collection & Integration
- Aggregated 10 years of financial statements, market reports, and operational data
- Integrated multiple data sources into a unified analytical framework

### 2. Exploratory & Statistical Analysis
- Applied regression analysis to identify drivers of revenue and margin performance
- Used statistical benchmarking to compare KPIs against industry averages
- Correlation analysis between investment decisions and financial outcomes

### 3. Machine Learning Components
- Regression models to forecast future financial performance based on historical trends
- Classification algorithms to segment markets and customer groups by profitability
- Anomaly detection to flag unusual cost or revenue patterns

### 4. Dashboard Development
- Built interactive visualisations in **Power BI** for executive consumption
- Cross-validated insights using **QlikView** for multi-dimensional analysis
- Structured reports in **MS Excel** for granular financial modelling

---

## 📈 Key Findings & Recommendations

- **Cost Efficiency Gap:** Production cost per unit was 12–15% above industry median — targeted supply chain optimisation recommended
- **R&D Investment Lag:** Competitors investing 2× more in EV/technology R&D — strategic realignment advised to maintain long-term competitiveness
- **Regional Opportunities:** Emerging market segments (Asia-Pacific) showed consistently higher growth rates, underrepresented in the company's portfolio mix
- **Pricing Strategy:** Premium segment pricing showed the strongest margin retention; mid-market segment under pressure from competitor discounting
